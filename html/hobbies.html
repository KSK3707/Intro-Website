There's nothing more satisfying than a good meal, right? Wrong. The most satisfying thing is making a good meal. And then boasting about it. Repeatedly.

One could say that maybe I'm exaggerating how nice it is to cook, but one would be wrong because once you get the hang of it, it's absolutely amazing. Of course, until you get the hang of it, it can be slightly...challenging. To say the least.

I have been told multiple times, repeatedly, that showing people food without actually giving it to them is 'mean' or something, but I'm just trying to show people what they're missing. And will probably continue to miss. Unless under very specific circumstances...okay, yeah, maybe I see the point that's being made, but I will maintain that seeing food is still a pretty good deal, even if you can't taste it.

Here, enjoy a curated selection of pictures of food I've made...at some point. Timestamps are unavailable, but did you really want them?

Some call crocheting an old lady hobby. To which I say 'Just because it takes a long time doesn't make it an old lady hobby please stop calling me Grandma I'm not that old yet also I'm gonna go take a nap in a nice spot of sunlight wake me up next week'

For legal reasons, most of that was a joke. Although there is something rather alluring about napping in sunlight. Does the idea that you're not supposed to sleep in light make it 'forbidden' and, by extension, more tantalizing? Who knows? Cats.

Have you ever met a cat? Yeah, no, me neither, but every time I see one it looks like it knows something I don't. Maybe it's the secrets of the universe. Maybe it's Maybelline.

Right, I got slightly off-track. Anyways, crochet. It's nice, if a bit monotonous. The bonus here is that you get cute things that you can give to your friends as gifts or use yourself as bag tags, plushies, clothing, etc. And the repetitive motion makes it rather soothing, so it's kinda like meditation. I think. I've never really managed to meditate successfully somehow.

Side Note: Whatever you do, don't lose count of your stitches it's painful and you end up backtracking so much my god just don't do it.
